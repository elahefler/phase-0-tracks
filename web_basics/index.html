Some Things About Cats

I decided to make this page about CATS because the assignment suggested it as a theme. Plus, I think cats are pretty cool (even though I am really more of a dog person). Here are a couple things that make cats ok:

  • You can name them after after anything and it makes a good name. This is not true for dogs
  • They have furry bellies
  • You don't have to take them on walks
